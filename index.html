Biography

Edward Hopper was born on July 22 1882 in Nyack, New York, USA. He signed his first oil painting at the age of 13. At the age of 18, Hopper studied at the New York School of Arts for 6 years. He learned illustration and painting with Robert Henri and Kenneth Hayes Miller.

Great art is the outward expression of an inner life in the artist, and this inner life will result in his personal vision of the world.
